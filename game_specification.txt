Musical Chairs Game Specification

Setup:
- 2 columns of 5 chairs (10 total), same color
- 10 players as different colored circles, start sitting on chairs (one player per chair)
- Eliminated players column on the side of the screen

Game Loop:
- Button click starts music, players circulate in oval path around chairs, one chair removed from bottom row (color changes to match background)
- Button click stops music, players continue moving until they reach the next chair position
- Odd chairs/even players: player at removed chair position is eliminated
- Even chairs/odd players: player not next to a chair position is eliminated
- Eliminated player moves to side column
- Remaining players sit on chairs (circle appears on top of chair) until next round
- Repeat until one winner remains

Visual Elements:

- Player colors: 10 different colors
- Background color: to be chosen
- Animation speed: to be chosen
- Music: mp3 file to be provided later
- Window size: to be chosen
- No special visual for winner
